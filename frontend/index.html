Rework & Modifications

No change is too small or difficult for us. We offer comprehensive modification services to bring your PCBs to life exactly as you envision them.

Component Replacement

Expert replacement of electronic components, including ICs, capacitors, resistors, and specialized modules. We handle both SMD and through-hole components with precision and care.

Wiring & Jumper Modifications

Custom wiring, jumper placement, and trace modifications. Whether you need circuit corrections, signal routing changes, or prototype customization, we've got you covered.

PCB Layout Adjustments

We can modify PCB layouts to accommodate design changes, add test points, update silkscreen, or implement design revisions. Perfect for prototype iterations.

Desoldering & Reballing

Professional desoldering of BGA, QFP, and other fine-pitch components with reballing capability. Ideal for component recovery and refurbishment.
